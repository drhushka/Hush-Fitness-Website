Personal Training

Our personal training sessions are not only focused on creating an individualized workout program, but we also focus on factors that will impact the client outside our training. Below, you will find more information about our personal training sessions! Our training sessions start at $35 per session (1 hr).

    Pre-training Screening
  • Conduct pre-participation screening, medical history, lifestyle questionnaire, and fitness assessment
  • Develop custom workouts with both short and long term goals
  • Present personalized exercise and physical activity guidelines
  • Debunk fitness myths and shift focus to a realistic approach to training
    During Session
  • Monitor your form to prevent injury and enhance overall fitness
  • Motivate you to push yourself to your potential
  • Set the tone for your workout by bringing your own music
  • How do we breakdown our hour training: we will include a 10 minute warm-up, followed by 40 minute workout, and ending with 10 minutes of static stretching
    Nutrition
  • Evaluate current eating plans and provide guidance
  • Promote USDA's MyPlate caloric guidelines
  • Encourage food tracking to identify areas of improvement
  • Focus on the significance of a balanced intake of macronutrients (carbohydrates, fats, and protein)
    Additional Information
  • $35/session (Sessions last one hour) *Rate is a starting rate and ONLY applies to training at the trainers home*
  • Includes Personalized Workout Programs shared with the client through Google Drive
