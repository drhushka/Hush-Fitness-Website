Mission Statement

Hush Fitness provides guidance and motivation to focus on your health and fitness goals through personal training! By creating individualized workout plans, we will focus on building healthy habits. Hush Fitness wants to be a part of your wellness journey! We believe that we need to focus more aspects your health and fitness to help you reach your goals. Training is important! However, many goals will not be complete without tackling your nutriton, mentality, and fitness to help were you want to be!

What is Hush Fitness?

Hush Fitness was created by Daniel in July 2022. He built the company to help clients achieve their fitness goals by focusing on all aspects of wellness. There are 8 dimensions of wellness: Physical, Occupational, Social, Spiritual, Intellectual, Environmental, Financial, and Emotional. Exercising is great! But let's focus on all the aspects of your health, so we can create a new way of healthy living and fitness while hitting your goals!

Background

Daniel received his B.S in Kinesiology with a minor in Exercise Science from Iowa State University. He is a Certified Nutrition Coach, Weight Loss Specialist, Corrective Exercise Specialist, Personal Trainer, and is working towards certification as a Strength and Conditioning Coach. In 2023, Daniel will pursue a Doctorate of Physical Therapy.
